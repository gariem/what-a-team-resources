# Child Safety Standards

**Effective date:** April 10, 2026

Emilio Rios ("we", "us", "our") is committed to the safety and protection of children. This page describes the child safety standards for **What A Team**.

## About the App

"What A Team" is a workplace team recognition app designed for adult users in professional settings. The app is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction).

## Our Commitment

We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any content that exploits or endangers children. We are committed to complying with all applicable child safety laws and will report any such content to the relevant national and regional authorities.

## How to Report a Concern

If you encounter any content or behavior within What A Team that you believe may be harmful to children, please report it immediately:

- **Email:** Contact us via the details on the [Impressum](impressum) page
- **GitHub:** [Open an issue](https://github.com/gariem/what-a-team-resources/issues)

All reports will be reviewed promptly and acted upon in accordance with applicable law.

## Content Moderation

What A Team allows users to send short recognition messages ("High Fives") within their team. We reserve the right to remove any content and terminate any account that violates these standards.

## Contact

For questions about our child safety practices, contact us via the details on the [Impressum](impressum) page.
