# Delete Your Account

**Effective date:** April 7, 2026

You can permanently delete your **What A Team** account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to do it and what happens when you do.

## Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly from the app:

1. Open **What A Team** and sign in.
2. Go to the **Profile** tab.
3. Tap **"Delete my account"**.
4. Confirm the action.

The deletion is **immediate** and cannot be undone.

## Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app (for example, you've uninstalled it or lost access to your sign-in method), you can request deletion by email:

- **Send an email to:** [raphsoft@gmail.com](mailto:raphsoft@gmail.com?subject=Account%20Deletion%20Request)
- **Subject line:** `Account Deletion Request`
- **From:** the email address associated with your account
- **Include:** the display name on your account, so we can verify your identity

Requests submitted by email are processed within **30 days**. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

## What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following is permanently removed:

- Your **profile** — display name, email, authentication identifier, and profile timestamps
- Your **team membership** — you are removed from any team you belong to
- **High Fives you've sent and received** — including the value, message text, sender, recipient, and timestamps
- Your **notification token** — used to deliver push notifications
- Any **team you created** as the sole creator, including its configuration, members, and full High Five history

## What We Retain

We may retain:

- **Anonymized, aggregated data** that cannot be linked back to you
- Records we are **legally required to keep** under applicable law

We do not retain personal data beyond what is strictly necessary.
